## Detailed Company Case Studies

### 1. Klarna (Customer Service Automation)
**Implementation**: AI assistant handling customer support
**Results**:
- Handled **2.3 million conversations** in first month
- Managed **two-thirds** of incoming support chats
- Reduced average resolution time from **11 minutes to under 2 minutes**
- Equivalent to **700 FTE capacity**
- **$40 million profit improvement** estimated for 2024

### 2. Synthesia (via Intercom's Fin AI Agent)
**Implementation**: Customer support automation
**Results**:
- Saved **1,300+ support hours** in six months
- Resolved over **6,000 conversations**
- During 690% volume spike: **98.3%** of users self-served without human escalation

### 3. Esusu (Email Support Automation)
**Implementation**: Email-based customer interaction automation
**Results**:
- Automated **64%** of email-based customer interactions
- **10-point CSAT improvement**
- **64%** faster first reply time
- **34%** shorter resolution time
- ~10,000 tickets/month with **80%** one-touch responses
